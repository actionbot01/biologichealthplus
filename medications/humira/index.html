Humira FAQ

How much does Humira cost without insurance?

Humira's list price is $7,500–$13,400/month. Through myAbbVie Assist, most commercially insured patients pay $0/month. Uninsured patients may receive it free through the patient assistance program.

Is there a Humira coupon or savings card?

Yes — myAbbVie Assist provides a co-pay savings card for commercially insured patients, reducing out-of-pocket costs to as low as $0/month.

What is the difference between Humira and its biosimilars?

Biosimilars like Hadlima and Hyrimoz are FDA-approved as highly similar to Humira. They often cost less and are covered preferentially by some insurance plans.

How is Humira administered?

Humira is given as a subcutaneous (under-the-skin) injection every 2 weeks. It comes as a pre-filled pen or syringe. BiologicHealthPlus provides injection training support.
